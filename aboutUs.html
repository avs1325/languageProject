Who we are

Serenity - an association created by 4 teenagers. The roots of our tree, our simple, innovative idea; was first born a few years back. At first, it was a seemingly small pitch for our English Language term project, a creative venture. And yet, that's where most opportunities rise - at a simple idea, a creative venture. With great thought, contemplation, and reflection, we finally decided to journey further; upwards and beyond! It is with utmost appreciation we say that we are infinitely grateful to our English Language teacher - Mr. Suryasubha Bannerjee - without whom we would not have been able to water the roots of our tree, without whom, we would not have been able to grow.

Why we do this

When we took our first small steps as an association, everything appeared strangely new to us,in a manner that caused us to be both anxious, and excited. The first steps gave us a view into the window of the world out there, aspects that we had yet to learn about. We could not be happier of our progress, of creating a platform which helps us to provide utmost ease for others to give; to donate to causes of their choices and interest.
We abundantly thank those that helped us, our journey would not have been possible if it wasn't for everybody out there contributing their time and effort for those in unfortunate circumstances, for showing so much compassion and concern. Because of all of you, we were able to progress; from little steps to big leaps; from stubby roots to ample trees. We ask of you one last thing. Please, help us grow further; help those out in dire need. Help us to change, to improve, to save lives.
